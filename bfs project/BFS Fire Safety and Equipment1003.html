Frequently Asked Questions

How often should fire extinguishers be refilled?

Fire extinguishers should be refilled every 6 month depending on type and usage. Monthly visual inspections and annual professional checks are recommended.

What makes BFS fire suppression balls different?

Our suppression balls are UL certified with automatic heat activation. They require no electrical connections and cover a 5-meter radius when deployed. Perfect for hard-to-reach areas.

Do you offer emergency services?

No, we do not offer emergency services at this time. Our focus is on fire safety equipment sales and maintenance.

Are your technicians certified?

All BFS technicians hold current Addis Ababa City Administration Fire & Disaster Risk Management Commission. certifications and undergo annual training to maintain industry proficiency.
